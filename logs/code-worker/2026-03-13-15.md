  - **collision detection**: tokenizes query + username, flags results where match comes only from username overlap. Hard collision (all tokens username-only) + soft collision (any overlap + low body relevance)

  - **discard logic**: keep=false for hard collisions, soft collisions, stale seen authors below relevance threshold, or low combined score
